Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

1. Introduction

Remi ("we", "our", "the Service") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store your information.

2. Data We Collect

  • Phone number: used to identify your account and deliver messages via WhatsApp.
  • Messages: the content of your conversations with Remi, used to process your requests and improve AI responses.
  • Location: city/coordinates you provide, used for weather and location-aware features.
  • Reminders and events: data you create within the Service.
  • Payment data: processed by Stripe. We do not store card details.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the Service.
  • To send reminders, briefings, and notifications you request.
  • To process subscription payments.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting support.

5. Third-Party Services

Remi uses the following third-party services:

  • WhatsApp / Twilio: message delivery
  • OpenAI: AI processing of messages
  • Stripe: payment processing
  • TikTok: marketing analytics (aggregated, not linked to individual user data)

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your data. Contact: privacy@remi.app

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.
